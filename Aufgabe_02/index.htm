Plant Care Tips

Taking care of your plants is easier than you might think. The key factors are water, light, and soil. Always check the soil moisture before watering, and avoid overwatering, as it is the most common cause of plant problems.

Make sure to place sun-loving plants near a window, while shade-loving plants should be kept away from direct sunlight. Re-potting once a year ensures your plant has enough space to grow.
